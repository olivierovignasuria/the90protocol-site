Privacy

The 90 Protocol is built to keep your work yours. Read this in plain terms.

Who is responsible (data controller)

The data controller for this site is Vigna Suria Oliviero (sole trader), Corso Siracusa 41, 10136 Torino, Italy, VAT IT13181900013. For any question or request about your data, contact oliviero.vignasuria@gmail.com.

Your answers stay on your device

This tool runs entirely in your web browser. Everything you type into it is saved only in your browser's local storage, on your own device. We do not collect it, transmit it, store it on a server, or have any way to read it. There is no account and no login.

What we do not do

  • No tracking cookies, no advertising pixels, no third-party analytics (no Google Analytics, no Clarity, nothing loaded from another company).
  • No selling or sharing of your data.
  • No sending your notes anywhere. The post-mortem, the runway numbers, the personal reflections: they never leave your browser.

Anonymous, cookieless usage analytics

To see whether the tool is helping and where people get stuck, we count anonymous usage events on our own servers (first-party, no third party involved). An event is just a name and which screen it happened on, for example "a tool was opened" or "the unlock button was clicked". We never send, and never receive, the answers you type, your name, your runway numbers, or any personal detail. There are no cookies: each browser tab gets a random throwaway id that is forgotten the moment you close the tab. If your browser sends a Do-Not-Track signal, we send nothing at all.

Booking a call (a third party)

If you choose to book a strategy call, that link takes you off this tool to a separate booking page run by Kim Calvert's team (Dynamite Lifestyle). Any details you enter on that page are handled by them, under their own privacy policy, to arrange and run the call. We do not pass your tool answers to them; you decide what to share on their page. The call discusses Kim's paid program, and if you choose to go further we earn a referral fee. We tell you all of this on screen before you click, so booking is a choice and not a surprise. The instrument is yours whether or not you book.

If you give us your email

Some tools let you optionally enter your email (for example, to receive your Gap Map and a short Follow-Through Week series). If you do, we store your email and the details you choose to send, on the basis of your consent, only to send you those messages. These emails include occasional offers for our paid tools and for Kim Calvert's program. You can withdraw consent and unsubscribe with one click in any email, which removes you from the list. We do not sell or share this data. Sending is handled by our email provider, Resend, acting as a processor on our behalf.

Deleting your data

Because your work lives only in your browser's local storage, you control it. Clearing your browser data for this site, or using your browser's "clear site data" option, removes it permanently. We cannot delete it for you because we do not have it.

Payment

If you buy the full kit, the payment is handled by our payment processor (Stripe). They process your payment details under their own privacy policy. We do not see or store your card details.
